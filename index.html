About Me

Certified Technician with over two years of experience currently going to school to gain more engineering knowledge. Before i found my talent as a Technician i was a Mechanic. Every sunny day i wasn't working was most likely spent in the garage working on my ride. I'm not gunna lie i did get frusterated at some points, but no matter how difficult it got nothing took away the satisfaction of fixing the code at the end of the day. I like to test my work at Sanoma Raceway to drag race. Before my drag racing days i did a lot of photography. People admired my photos and asked to do photoshoots of them with their race cars. When i'm not racing or at school i'm most likely taking a walk with my dogs. My first job was with Pep Boys in Commercial Sales. I was able to use my car knowledge and apply them at work, but as a female i found out quickly that my knowledge was questioned. After a while i got annoyed of peoples opinion and figured i need to change my field to something where my knowledge wouldn't be questioned and valued regardless of the sex. I joined a nine month program at Unitek College and went over Hardware, Software, Networking, Security, Vlan, Server, Cloud and Desk Support. I graduated with a Certification of Completion in Information Technology. That helped me get my foot into Google through a vendor company. After a year of being with Google I learned that a lot of big companies like Facebook and Google hire most of their workers through vendor companies which are highly underpaid compared to white badge Google Employees. I found it hard as a vendor to be able to grow in the company, so i was motivated to go back to school so i can be able to apply to any engineering position. My goal after this boot camp is to apply back to Google and become a white badge employee. So far in this boot camp i've learned soo much and can't wait to master the art and become a professional developer.
